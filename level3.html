Level 3: How it spreads

How COVID-19 spreads:

    People who are close to another person can get infected with COVID-19.
    When people with COVID-19 cough, sneeze, sing, talk, or breathe they give off small, water droplets like rain that can infect others.
    A person can get COVID-19 from the virus that stays in the air for a while.
    A person could get COVID-19 by touching a surface or object that has the virus on it and then touching their own mouth, nose, or eyes.
